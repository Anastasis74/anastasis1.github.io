About

I am a research mathematician working at the intersection of algebraic geometry, number theory, and representation theory. My current focus lies in the cohomology of moduli spaces, enumerative geometry, and the arithmetic of Shimura varieties. I received my Ph.D. from Princeton University in 2020, advised by Prof. A. Smith, and held postdoctoral positions at the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics and University of Cambridge.

I am passionate about bridging abstract theory with explicit computations, and I enjoy collaborating across disciplines.
